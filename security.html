Security at vestedTM

At vestedTM, the security of your data is our highest priority. All sensitive account information is protected with industry standard encryption, multi-factor authentication, and more.

Exceeding the industry standard

Not only does vested use the same security measures as major banks, but our system is read-only. That means we protect your privacy by never doing more than reading financial data in aggregate form.

  • We do not store your account login information such as usernames or passwords, they are encrypted and used only once to establish a connection to your bank
  • Nor do we ever request access to money in your account or confidential information such as credit card, account, or routing numbers.
  • Also we're not in the business of sharing or selling your data to anyone for any reason. We succeed when your workplace is financially healthier and that means protecting your financial privacy at all costs.
