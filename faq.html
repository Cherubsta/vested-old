Frequently Asked Questions

What is vested?

vested helps you save money for the things you care about, by learning your finances, recommending goals that are in your reach, and showing you the path forward.

Is vested right for me?

If you are in debt and want to dig yourself out, or if you're debt–free and want to make the most of your extra earnings, you came to the right place.

How much does it cost to use?

vested is free to use!

Are you a bank?

We're not a bank. Think of vested?, as a financial advisor, letting you know your options, and keeping you track to reaching your goal.

Where do my savings go?

Since we’re not a bank, it’s up to you to move the money you save into a dedicated account. This would help you keep track of it. Or if you are investing it, we'll recommend long-term savings options such as 401K or Roth IRA or shorter term savings such as a high-interest online banking account.

What if I use a joint account?

Call Fadi.

How do you keep my data secure?

To protect your financial security and privacy, vested uses the same level of security as your bank. We never have access to money in your account or confidential information such as full credit card, account or routing numbers, and we will not share or sell your data to anyone for any reason.
